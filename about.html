core values
—
Efflux's core values are focus on talent development, passion, adaptability, thinking big, and learning from our mistakes. We seek to create sustainable success and uphold the integrity of Efflux’s brand through these values, which have been shaped by our experiences, lessons, and challenges.

Talent Development

We aim to create the best conditions to develop individual expertise, and uphold the company’s overall culture while respecting individual differences.

Passion

At Efflux, we always have passion for all working related technologies. Passion is what powers us to complete every mission happily.

Adaptability

The technology industry changes every day. Efflux continually pushes forward to adapt to new trends and technology. We pride ourselves on our constant evolution to remain at the forefront of our field.

Thinking Big

Efflux creates each product with the end goal of global use and distribution. However, our big dreams are always homegrown and carefully created, step by step.

Learning From Mistakes

Efflux is not afraid of making mistakes. We believe that mistakes give us learning opportunities that help us to grow. Failures are inevitable on your way to success, and Efflux seeks to embrace this.
